Full description (paste everything from the next line until the --- end marker)
---
ARCHI is your community hub for local news, classifieds, and conversation — in the language you choose.

Whether you want to stay informed, sell or find something, offer or hire services, browse jobs, or talk in the general chat, ARCHI keeps it in one calm, dark-themed experience on Android (and the same account works on the web where supported).

WHAT YOU CAN DO
• News — read and share posts; optional photos and video links (e.g. YouTube)
• Listings — buy, sell, rent, offer services, or find help across marketplace categories
• Jobs — post and explore vacancies with clear details
• Chat — public room for text, links, and media you choose to attach
• Comments — discuss posts and listings where enabled
• Language — switch the interface anytime; the app is built for a multilingual audience
• Safety — new accounts confirm a phone number by SMS to reduce spam and abuse (where SMS is configured)

PRIVACY & TRUST
We explain how we use data in our Privacy Policy on the website. Terms of Use describe the rules of the service. You can block users from your profile settings so you do not see their content.

ARCHI is for people who want real connection and clear offers — without juggling dozens of apps.
